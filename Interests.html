My main interest is art. I like most artistic things and I tend to draw a lot of people portraits.
I draw a lot of celebrities portraits and each portrait, depending on the quality can take between 2 - 15 Hours.

The materials I usually use for a portrait are

  • An HB mechanical pencil
  • A 2B pencil
  • A 4B pencil
  • A 6B pencil
  • An Eraser
  • A rough piece of paper which can grip pencil graphite
  • A charcoal pencil
  • A piece of toilet paper or a makeup brush for large areas of blending
  • A small eraser of some kind to do very small highlights
  • A small blending tool of any sort for tiny blending details
  • A black colouring pencil
